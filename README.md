# 🍃 Plantropic: B2B Industrial Water Management & ESG SaaS

Plantropic is a specialized B2B Software as a Service (SaaS) platform designed to help enterprise companies calculate their carbon footprint, compile ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) data, and manage sustainability programs efficiently and cost-effectively.

## Key Features

* **Real-Time Water & Resource Monitoring:** Designed with IoT-ready architecture to ingest real-time telemetry data from industrial sensors (e.g., flow meters, microcontrollers), allowing for automated tracking and leak detection.
* **Smart ESG Data Management:** Streamlines the collection and structuring of environmental data, ensuring compliance with international sustainability reporting standards.
* **Advanced Data Analytics:** Built with scalable data structures, enabling advanced SQL querying and preparing the groundwork for predictive machine learning models to forecast resource consumption and minimize waste.
* **Carbon Footprint Calculator:** Automated tools to measure operational carbon output across various industrial scopes.

## Business Impact
For large-scale operations in tech, energy, or FMCG sectors, manual ESG reporting and unmonitored water usage lead to compliance risks and financial drain. Plantropic solves this by acting as a centralized, data-driven command center—turning raw environmental metrics into actionable business intelligence that reduces operational costs and boosts corporate sustainability ratings.

## Tech Stack
* **Framework:** Next.js (App Router)
* **Language:** TypeScript
* **Styling:** Tailwind CSS
